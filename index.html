MUSEUM OF CANDY

The Museum of Candy is a whimsical and immersive experience that takes visitors on a journey through the fascinating history and culture of candy. Located in the heart of downtown, the museum showcases a colorful array of exhibits, interactive displays, and tantalizing sweet treats that are sure to delight visitors of all ages.Step back in time and learn about the origins of candy, from ancient civilizations to the modern-day confectionery industry. Explore vintage candy wrappers, advertisements, and artifacts that highlight the evolution of sweet treats throughout history.

LOLLIPOPS IN CANDY

Lollipops, those delightful sweet treats on a stick, have a fascinating history that stretches back centuries. From their humble beginnings to their iconic status in modern culture, lollipops have captured the hearts and taste buds of people around the world.As sugar became more readily available in the Middle Ages, candy-making techniques advanced, leading to the development of more sophisticated lollipop recipes. By the 17th century, hard candies were being molded into various shapes, including balls and discs, and served on sticks for easier consumption.

GUMBALLS IN CANDY

Gumballs, those colorful and chewy treats found in vending machines and candy shops around the world, have been delighting people of all ages for generations.
